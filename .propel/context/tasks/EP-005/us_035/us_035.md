# User Story - US_035

## Story ID

- ID: US_035

## Story Title

- Reminder Scheduling Background Jobs

## Description

- As a patient, I want to receive a reminder 24 hours before my appointment (email + SMS) and 2 hours before (SMS only), so that I do not miss my scheduled visit.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** an appointment is scheduled for tomorrow, **When** the 24-hour reminder batch job runs, **Then** the system sends a personalized email and SMS with appointment details and cancellation link.
2. **Given** an appointment is scheduled in 2 hours, **When** the 2-hour reminder batch job runs, **Then** the system sends an SMS-only reminder to the patient.
3. **Given** a patient has opted out of SMS, **When** the 24-hour reminder job runs, **Then** only the email reminder is sent and SMS is skipped with "opted-out" logged.
4. **Given** the reminder batch job runs, **When** it processes all scheduled appointments, **Then** the entire batch completes within 10 minutes.
5. **Given** an appointment is cancelled before the reminder window, **When** the batch job processes it, **Then** the reminder is skipped and status logged as "cancelled-before-send."

## Edge Cases

- What happens when the reminder job fails mid-batch? System resumes from the last successful record on next run using checkpoint tracking.
- How are timezone differences handled? All reminder scheduling uses the clinic's local timezone; patient timezone is not tracked in Phase 1.
