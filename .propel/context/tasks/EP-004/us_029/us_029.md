# User Story - US_029

## Story ID

- ID: US_029

## Story Title

- AI-to-Manual Intake Mode Switching

## Description

- As a patient, I want to switch between AI conversational and manual form intake at any point without losing data, so that I can choose my preferred method mid-session.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I am in AI intake mode, **When** I click "Switch to Manual Form", **Then** the manual form loads with all previously collected data pre-filled.

2. **Given** I am in manual form mode, **When** I click "Switch to AI Intake", **Then** the AI chat resumes with context of previously entered data and continues from the next uncollected field.

3. **Given** I switch modes, **When** the transition completes, **Then** no data is lost and all previously provided answers are preserved.

4. **Given** I switch modes multiple times, **When** I eventually submit, **Then** the final intake contains the merged data from all modes with correct source attribution.

## Edge Cases

- What happens when conflicting data exists between AI and manual entries? Most recent entry takes precedence; conflict is noted in intake record.
- How does the system handle switching when the AI service is unavailable? Switch-to-AI button is disabled; alert shows "AI intake temporarily unavailable."
