# User Story - US_040

## Story ID

- ID: US_040

## Story Title

- AI Clinical Data Extraction (Medications, Diagnoses, Procedures, Allergies)

## Description

- As a staff member, I want the AI to extract medications, diagnoses, procedures, and allergies from parsed clinical documents with source attribution, so that patient data is structured and traceable to original sources.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** a clinical document is parsed, **When** AI extraction completes, **Then** the system extracts medication records including drug name, dosage, frequency, and prescribing physician.
2. **Given** a clinical document is parsed, **When** AI extraction completes, **Then** the system extracts diagnosis information with associated dates and treating providers.
3. **Given** a clinical document is parsed, **When** AI extraction completes, **Then** the system extracts procedure history with dates, descriptions, and performing physicians.
4. **Given** a clinical document is parsed, **When** AI extraction completes, **Then** the system extracts allergy information including allergen, reaction type, and severity.
5. **Given** any data point is extracted, **When** it is stored in ExtractedData, **Then** the source document ID, page number, and extraction region are recorded for attribution.

## Edge Cases

- What happens when the AI cannot identify any structured data in a document? System marks the document as "no-data-extracted" and flags for manual review.
- How does the system handle documents in languages other than English? Phase 1 supports English only; non-English documents are flagged as "unsupported-language."
