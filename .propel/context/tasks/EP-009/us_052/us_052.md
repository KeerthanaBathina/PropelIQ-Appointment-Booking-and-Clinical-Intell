# User Story - US_052

## Story ID

- ID: US_052

## Story Title

- Patient Arrival Status Marking

## Description

- As a staff member, I want to mark patient arrival status (arrived, no-show, cancelled) with automatic wait time calculation, so that the arrival queue accurately reflects real-time patient flow.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** a patient arrives at the facility, **When** the staff member marks them as "arrived," **Then** the system records the arrival timestamp and begins calculating wait time.
2. **Given** a patient's appointment time passes without arrival, **When** 15 minutes elapse, **Then** the system automatically marks the patient as "no-show" and updates the queue entry.
3. **Given** a patient cancels at the front desk, **When** the staff member marks them as "cancelled," **Then** the status updates immediately and the slot is released for walk-ins.
4. **Given** a patient is marked as arrived, **When** wait time is displayed, **Then** it is calculated in real-time from the arrival timestamp to current time.

## Edge Cases

- What happens when a patient arrives after being auto-marked as no-show? Staff can override the no-show status to "arrived-late," which is logged in the audit trail.
- How does the system handle duplicate arrival markings? System prevents duplicate marking with an error "Patient already marked as arrived at [timestamp]."
