# User Story - US_055

## Story ID

- ID: US_055

## Story Title

- Auto No-Show Detection and Wait Time Alerts

## Description

- As a staff member, I want the system to automatically mark patients as no-show after 15 minutes and alert me when any patient has been waiting beyond a configurable threshold, so that I can proactively manage patient flow and appointment gaps.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** a patient has not been marked as arrived, **When** 15 minutes pass after their scheduled appointment time, **Then** the system automatically marks the appointment as "no-show" and updates the queue.
2. **Given** a patient has been waiting, **When** their wait time exceeds the configurable threshold (default 30 minutes), **Then** the system displays a visual alert (amber/red badge) on the queue entry and notifies the staff member.
3. **Given** the wait time threshold is configurable, **When** an admin changes the threshold value, **Then** the alert behavior updates immediately for all active queue entries.
4. **Given** auto no-show detection runs, **When** a patient is marked as no-show, **Then** the event is logged in the audit trail with timestamp and "auto-detected" attribution.

## Edge Cases

- What happens when no-show detection runs during a system outage recovery? System processes all overdue appointments retroactively on recovery, marking them with "delayed-detection" flag.
- How does the system handle patients who check in at 14 minutes (just before auto no-show)? The 15-minute timer resets upon arrival marking; auto no-show is cancelled.
