# User Story - US_026

## Story ID

- ID: US_026

## Story Title

- No-Show Risk Score Calculation

## Description

- As a staff member, I want the system to calculate and display a no-show risk score for each appointment, so that I can proactively manage high-risk appointments and reduce no-show rates.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** an appointment exists, **When** the risk score is calculated, **Then** it produces a score from 0-100 based on patient history (past no-shows, cancellations) and appointment characteristics (time of day, day of week).

2. **Given** risk scores are available, **When** staff views the appointment list, **Then** each appointment displays its risk score with color coding (green <30, amber 30-69, red >=70).

3. **Given** insufficient historical data (new patient, <3 appointments), **When** the risk score is calculated, **Then** the system uses rule-based defaults and displays "Estimated" label.

4. **Given** risk scores are computed, **When** the system evaluates slot swap priority, **Then** lower no-show risk patients are prioritized for preferred slots.

## Edge Cases

- What happens when a patient's risk score changes after a completed visit? Score recalculates on next appointment creation incorporating the updated history.
- How does the system handle a patient with 100% no-show history? Score caps at 100; staff alert is triggered for manual outreach.
