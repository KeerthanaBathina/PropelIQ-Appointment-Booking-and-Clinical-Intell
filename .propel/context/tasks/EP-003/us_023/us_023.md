# User Story - US_023

## Story ID

- ID: US_023

## Story Title

- Appointment Rescheduling

## Description

- As a patient, I want to reschedule my appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time, so that I can move to a more convenient slot without cancelling and rebooking.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I have a scheduled appointment, **When** I select "Reschedule" and choose a new slot, **Then** the old slot is released and the new appointment is created atomically within 2 seconds.

2. **Given** the new appointment time is within 24 hours of the original, **When** I attempt to reschedule, **Then** the system rejects with "Cannot reschedule within 24 hours of appointment."

3. **Given** rescheduling succeeds, **When** the confirmation displays, **Then** it shows both the original and new appointment times with a success message.

4. **Given** I reschedule, **When** the transaction completes, **Then** updated confirmation is sent via email and SMS, and my calendar sync updates.

## Edge Cases

- What happens when the new slot becomes unavailable during rescheduling? Optimistic locking catches the conflict; user sees "Slot no longer available" with refreshed options.
- How does the system handle rescheduling a walk-in appointment? Walk-in appointments cannot be rescheduled by patients; only staff can modify.
