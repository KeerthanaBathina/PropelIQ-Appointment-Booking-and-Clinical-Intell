# User Story - US_020

## Story ID

- ID: US_020

## Story Title

- Waitlist Registration & Notification

## Description

- As a patient, I want to register for a waitlist when my preferred slot is fully booked, so that I am automatically notified when a slot opens up.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** all slots for my preferred time are booked, **When** I select "Join Waitlist", **Then** I am registered on the waitlist with my preferred criteria (date, time, provider).

2. **Given** I am on the waitlist, **When** a matching slot becomes available, **Then** I receive a notification (email + SMS) within 5 minutes of slot availability.

3. **Given** I receive a waitlist notification, **When** I click the booking link, **Then** the slot is held for me for 1 minute to complete booking.

4. **Given** multiple patients are waitlisted for the same slot, **When** the slot opens, **Then** the system notifies all waitlisted patients and the first to confirm books the slot.

## Edge Cases

- What happens when I'm waitlisted but cancel my existing appointment? Waitlist entry remains active unless explicitly removed.
- How does the system handle a waitlist slot that opens within 24 hours? System still notifies but notes the upcoming time.
