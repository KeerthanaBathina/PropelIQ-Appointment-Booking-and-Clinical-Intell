# User Story - US_021

## Story ID

- ID: US_021

## Story Title

- Dynamic Preferred Slot Swap

## Description

- As a patient with preferred slot criteria, I want the system to automatically move me to a preferred slot when one becomes available, so that I get a better appointment time without manual rebooking.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I have an appointment and registered preferred slot criteria, **When** a matching preferred slot opens (cancellation or new availability), **Then** the system automatically swaps my appointment to the preferred slot.

2. **Given** the swap occurs, **When** it completes, **Then** my original slot is released within 1 minute and I receive a notification with old and new appointment times.

3. **Given** staff has disabled auto-swap for my account, **When** a preferred slot opens, **Then** the system skips the swap and logs the reason.

4. **Given** multiple patients prefer the same slot, **When** the slot opens, **Then** the system prioritizes by longest wait time and lowest no-show risk score.

5. **Given** a preferred slot opens less than 24 hours before the appointment, **When** the system evaluates the swap, **Then** it skips automatic swap and notifies me for manual confirmation instead.

## Edge Cases

- What happens when a concurrent swap creates a conflict? Optimistic locking prevents double-booking; failed swap retries for next preferred slot.
- How does the system handle swap when the patient has already checked in? Swap is skipped for appointments with arrival status "arrived" or "in-visit".
