# User Story - US_019

## Story ID

- ID: US_019

## Story Title

- Appointment Cancellation & Slot Release

## Description

- As a patient, I want to cancel my appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time, so that the slot becomes available for other patients promptly.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I have a scheduled appointment, **When** I cancel more than 24 hours before the appointment, **Then** the appointment status changes to "cancelled" and the slot is released within 1 minute.

2. **Given** I attempt to cancel within 24 hours of the appointment, **When** I submit the cancellation, **Then** the system displays "Cancellations within 24 hours are not permitted. Please contact the clinic."

3. **Given** a slot is released after cancellation, **When** another patient views availability, **Then** the released slot appears as available within 1 minute of cancellation.

4. **Given** cancellation is confirmed, **When** the process completes, **Then** an audit log entry records the cancellation with user attribution and timestamp.

## Edge Cases

- What happens when a cancellation request is made for an already-cancelled appointment? System returns "This appointment has already been cancelled."
- How does the system handle timezone differences? All times are stored and compared in UTC; display converts to user's local timezone.
